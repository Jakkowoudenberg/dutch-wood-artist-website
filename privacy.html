Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026 · Dutch Wood Artist · KvK 51624842

This privacy policy explains how Dutch Wood Artist (operated by Jakko Woudenberg, registered in the Netherlands under KvK number 51624842, based in Schagen) collects, uses, and protects your personal data when you use this website.

1. Who we are

Dutch Wood Artist is a sole proprietorship (eenmanszaak) registered in the Netherlands. The owner and data controller is Jakko Woudenberg, reachable via the contact form on this website or by email at dutchwoodartist@gmail.com.

2. What data we collect

We only collect personal data that you voluntarily provide through the contact form on this website. This includes your name, email address, phone number, and any message you choose to send. We do not collect data automatically through cookies, tracking pixels, or analytics tools.

3. Why we collect your data

We collect your contact details solely to respond to your inquiry. Your data is never used for marketing without your explicit consent, never sold to third parties, and never shared with anyone outside of Dutch Wood Artist except where required by law.

4. How long we keep your data

We retain your contact information for as long as necessary to respond to your inquiry and for up to two years thereafter for administrative purposes. You may request deletion of your data at any time.

5. Where your data is stored

Contact form submissions are processed and stored by Netlify (netlify.com), a US-based hosting provider. Netlify complies with GDPR through standard contractual clauses. Notifications may also be forwarded via Zapier (zapier.com) to our email inbox. Both services are used solely for delivery and storage of your message.

6. Your rights (GDPR)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the right to access the personal data we hold about you, request correction or deletion of your data, object to processing, and request data portability. To exercise any of these rights, contact us via the contact form or at dutchwoodartist@gmail.com. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Dutch Data Protection Authority (Autoriteit Persoonsgegevens) at autoriteitpersoonsgegevens.nl.

7. Cookies

This website does not use cookies for tracking or analytics purposes. No consent banner is required. If this changes in the future, this policy will be updated accordingly.

8. Third-party links

This website contains links to external websites including thenightwatchinwood.com, app.unitedwoodfloorlayers.com, archdaily.com and others. Dutch Wood Artist is not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of the website constitutes acceptance of any changes.

10. Contact

For any questions about this privacy policy or your personal data, contact us via the contact form on dutchwoodartist.com or by email at dutchwoodartist@gmail.com.
